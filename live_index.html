Comprehensive Curriculum

This program is designed to take students from foundational styling concepts to advanced industry techniques. Throughout the 12 weeks, students will engage in interactive learning experiences that include hands-on assignments, real world case studies, mood boarding and portfolio development.

Flexible Learning

The course is structured to accommodate various learning paces, allowing students to balance their education with other commitments. Live sessions provide real-time engagement, while recorded lessons ensure accessibility at any time.
